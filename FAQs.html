Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine what supplements, nutritional, and fitness recommendations to send me?

Certain SNPs, or single nucleotide polymorphisms, in your DNA can indicate unique traits, such as the type of foods you metabolize better, possible nutritional deficiencies, low energy, slow metabolism, difficulty concentrating, and more. We then pair you with the ingredients, nutritional recommendations, diet and fitness strategies that have been shown to be effective for people with your particular genetic makeup..

Is my privacy protected?

When you upload your DNA to LifeDNA, you keep 100% ownership over your data.

We will never share it without your permission.

Our system relies on a bullet-proof, active moderation security method. This means that the only way to access your DNA is with your direct approval or request, double-checked by multiple humans on our security team.

Your data is encrypted, protected, and safe, guaranteed.

How much research have you done?

We have over 600 peer-reviewed studies showing the connection between genetic markers and the ingredients, nutritional recommendations, diet plans, fitness programs, and reports that we generate. You can check out our research here.

How accurate are my DNA reports?

With over 600 peer-reviewed scientific studies, our database of genetic research provides one of the most accurate and complete DNA profiles available anywhere online.

Your DNA only increases the chances of incurring particular traits. Your environment and behaviors will also have an effect. So while some genetic relationships are very obvious, most genetic traits will be influenced by other factors as well. Taking your lifestyle, habits, environment, and DNA into consideration will help you get a better understanding of yourself.

Can LifeDNA help me lose weight?

Yes! DNA-targeted weight loss programs have been shown to help people lose 33% more weight.

When you sign up for our DNA personalized meal and fitness plans, we create monthly meal plans based on your unique response to carbs, fats, proteins, and other nutrients. Then, we build monthly fitness routines using the best exercises for you to maximize your fat-burning and optimize your muscular improvements.

And of course, we take your personal goals into account so that you’re getting the results you really want from your LifeDNA Meal and Fitness Program.

How long does it take to get my results?

If you’re sending a LifeDNA DNA Test Kit, it takes about 2 weeks to process your results. If you’re uploading your DNA from 23andMe or Ancestry, your results will be generated instantly.

What makes LifeDNA different from other genetic testing companies?

Aside from focusing on your privacy and security, we work hard to ensure that our research database is full of validated and accurate studies. Then, we use that information to help you live a better, healthier, more fulfilling life.

That means creating custom meal and fitness plans, constantly tracking your progress, and updating your program to ensure that you’re always getting optimized results. Plus, you work with your very own LifeDNA Certified Genetic Health Expert to make sure that you’re still on track to achieve your goals. Our researchers, geneticists, nutritional experts, and coaches all work for you, helping you achieve more than ever before.
